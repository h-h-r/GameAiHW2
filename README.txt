Description:
	Casting rays to detect obstacles and receive hit point info therefore performing wall and tree avoidence during dynamic wander.
Dynamic wander:
	Same as HW1.
Wall avoidence:
	Calculate a new target position on the normal vector of the hit surface starting from hit point, then seek this new target.
Tree avoidence:
	Evade from the hit point on the tree.
